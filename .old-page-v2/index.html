Play the Demo

A metroidvania about time & decision making.




Features

Non-Lineal Exploration

Explore each barrier of temporal corruption and discover the different areas seeking to save your timeline, in a dreamlike, dark and corrupt world.

Self-Paced Combat

Attack, dash, parry and attack again! or just relax and explore the world and do some sidequest. Level up, learn new skills, meet new friends and enemies, or just go and fight every boss and complete the game as fast as possible.

It's your choice!

Completionist?

Discover the world where time stops, and explore this cursed vast lands full of different characters! Find over 100 quests, multiple companions and optional areas to test your skills while an epic pixel art & chiptune soundtrack follow you during your adventure.

Customize Yourself

Discover new skills, collect soul fragments, equip yourself with upgrades and change your weapon according to your combat style. Experiment with different combinations and discover the one you like the most.

Choose Your Side

Play 2 sides of the same story! Ebura's "Reminiscense" story and Keybold's "Council" story, two rival clans looking for the same but with different visions. Your choices will lead one of the clans to the victory.

Change The Future

Change our destiny, save your friends, kill your enemies, make a choice. Every action matters, every decision you make, every path you take, and every character you met, may change the future of this world and the length of our story. Would you get all the endings?
